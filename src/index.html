About Me

As a Solutions Engineer adept at seamlessly merging technical prowess with exceptional client interaction, I am dedicated to crafting optimized user experiences through code and creativity. My background extends from troubleshooting intricate technical challenges, refined during my tenure as an Audio Visual Technician, to proficiently collaborating with clients, bridging the gap between complex tech concepts through relatable terms. My versatility shines in web development, coupled with video engineering, photography, and technical event production.

I developed this expertise through my Computer Science degree from Carleton College, situated in picturesque, rural Northfield, Minnesota - a town affectionately known as the city of Cows, Colleges, and Contentment (I'm serious). With a multicultural heritage encompassing American and British cultures combined with the diversity of growing up in Los Angeles, California, I approach the ever-evolving technology landscape with a unique perspective. My journey from an inquisitive and technically inclined child to mastering complex code dependencies underscores my growth. My unyielding curiosity propels me to explore the nexus of technology, science, culture, and the wider world, reminiscent of the fascination with the every-day kindled by "How It's Made." This curiosity fuels my passions spanning space and astronomy, cybersecurity, live event production, client support, and sustainable energy solutions.

My experiences have endowed me with a profound appreciation for the delicate interplay between code, creativity, and client satisfaction. This diverse skill set positions me as a dynamic professional adept at crafting holistic solutions that seamlessly intertwine technology and human interaction.
